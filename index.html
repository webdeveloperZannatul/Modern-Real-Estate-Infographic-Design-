Design Guidelines

What to Avoid

  • Apartment towers or high-density buildings
  • Aggressive demolition imagery (bulldozers)
  • Cheesy real estate flyer graphics
  • Cartoon homes or generic AI blueprints
  • Flashy, bright marketing layouts

What to Aim For

  • Modern, minimal, and premium aesthetics
  • Architectural precision and intelligence
  • Calm, strategic visualizations
  • Subtle development overlays
  • Premium advisory / investment aesthetic

The Homeowner Realization

"The house is what's visible. The land may be where the hidden value lives."

Your design should make a homeowner curious, not pressured. Focus on the strategic intelligence of land valuation and the calm realization of potential.
